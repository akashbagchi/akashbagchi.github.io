Graduation Project | Research

Accessible Video Games

Young children, especially those on the autistic spectrum, often do not learn educational subjects and life skills in the same manner as neurotypicals. Recreational activities like games, puzzles and co-curriculars are known to provide additional opportunities for learning while also engaging the child. This project aimed to create a series of educational video games that are accessible to children with autism, and can be used as a tool for learning and development. Read the Research Paper here.

Web Technologies Project

University Student Resources Portal

The University Student Resources Portal, designed in this case for use in Dayananda Sagar University - is a fully responsive and modern web application built with HTML, Bootstrap, JavaScript, MySQL and PHP. The website provides attending students of the university with a wide host of services and resources. Class notes, important announcements, semester timetables, Club events and social media links, etc. It also provides admin & maintenance personnel the means to upload the aforementioned resources on-site with the use of a password-protected form mechanism.
